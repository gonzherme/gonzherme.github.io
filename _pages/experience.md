# Experience

## Rubrik - Software Engineering Intern
*June 2024 – August 2024*

![Rubrik Logo](images/rubrik-logo.png)

As a Software Engineering Intern at **Rubrik**, I contributed to the redesign of the **database service architecture**, which played a critical role in tracking **6000+ customer database instances** across **GCP clusters**. My efforts led to the creation of a new **DB KV-store service** via **gRPC**, successfully replacing a third-party service (Consul), saving the company **$8,000 annually**.

### Key Accomplishments:
- **Redesigned the KV-store interface** to enhance scalability and performance.
- **Developed backup and recovery processes** leveraging **GCP buckets** to improve operational efficiency and reliability.
- Optimized database service **cost and resource usage**, leading to significant savings.

#### Technologies Used:
- **GCP**, **gRPC**, **Consul**, **Database Design**, **Cloud Computing**, **Infrastructure Optimization**

My experience at Rubrik helped me deepen my understanding of cloud architectures and scalability while also giving me the opportunity to make a tangible impact on the company's infrastructure.
